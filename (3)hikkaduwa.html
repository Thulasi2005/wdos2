BABY TURTLES

Sea turtles are a threatened species. Human activity is the major cause of this. Nevertheless, there are organisations working to make things better. One of them is the turtle hatchery at Hikkaduwa. A short tuk tuk ride will take you from Hikkaduwa town to the turtle hatchery. There is a 500 LKR admission charge, and contributions are appreciated.


They are attempting to educate both residents and tourists and restore the population. When the young turtles are ready, they are finally released back into the water after being carefully looked over turtle eggs.


A terrific educational experience, visiting the centre lets you get near to the turtles without disrupting their ecosystem:


  • The facility has several tanks where you can witness the range of species swimming about, as well as a sandy area where the turtles hatch behind little hills.
  • For an extra cost, you can pay to have turtles returned to the water.
  • You may significantly aid in the recovery of these threatened species by visiting the centre.
